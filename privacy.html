Section x: Cookies

We may use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our site. Cookies are not obligatory for browsing but may be required for certain features. We use Google Analytics, which collects data about site usage. Please refer to our Cookies Policy for more information.

In connection with your use of the Site, we or our authorized service providers may employ cookies, web beacons, and other comparable technologies.

Accessing the Site does not necessitate accepting cookies. We would like to draw attention to the fact that using the Site's "basket" feature and placing orders is only feasible when cookies are enabled. Cookies are text files that are stored in your browser's memory or on your device when you visit a website or read a message. They are usually made up of letters and numbers. They enable us to identify a specific device or browser. Web beacons are tiny visual images that could be present on the website.

They help us better understand your preferences and interests by enabling us to count the number of users who have viewed these pages. When you visit specific pages on the Site, cookies—tiny text files that are saved to your computer's hard drive by your Internet browser—identify your computer to our server as a unique user. Your Internet Protocol address can be recognized by cookies, which will save you time when you visit or want to visit the website.

We don't collect or use any other information about you, such as targeted advertising; instead, we only use cookies to make your experience on the site easier. For example, cookies help us remember who you are when you want to change your shopping cart without having to enter your email address again. Please be aware, however, that altering or removing cookies may have an impact on the functionality that is available on the Sit. Some cookies are necessary to enable essential functionality (like adding items to your shopping basket). Although you could configure your browser to reject cookies, doing so would limit how you could use the site.

We assure you that the cookies we use are virus-free and do not contain any personal or private information. Visit all-about-cookies for additional information about cookies, or click this link to learn how to delete them from your browser.
